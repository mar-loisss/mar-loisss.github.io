About Theatre Guild

A passionate community of artists dedicated to creating unforgettable stage performances and nurturing young talents.

Our Story Meet the Team

Our Mission

Theatre Guild commits to nurturing natural and diverse talents and providing a venue for members to develop leadership, independence, teamwork, resourcefulness, and organizational skills through theatrical stage productions.

Our Vision

Theatre Guild envisions becoming a standard of excellence in the performing arts and a venue for the holistic cultural and human growth of its members.

Our History

Founded in 2017, Theatre Guild is a community-rooted company dedicated to producing new and classic plays. For over a decade, we've been a beacon of theatrical excellence.

Who We Are

Committed to excellence in performing arts

Building Theatre Excellence

Theatre Guild has been a cornerstone of artistic expression and community engagement. Our ensemble brings together talented performers, directors, and creative professionals who share a passion for storytelling and theatrical innovation.

We believe theatre is more than entertainment—it's a transformative experience that connects people, celebrates human diversity, and inspires growth. Through each production, we strive to create moments that resonate with audiences and empower our members to reach their full potential.

How to Join Theatre Guild

Everyone is welcome—on stage or behind the scenes

Step 1 — Sign Up

Fill out our interest form and tell us if you want to perform, design, work backstage, or help with events.

Step 2 — Meet the Guild

Attend workshops, orientations, and open rehearsals to get to know the officers, members, and our creative process.

Step 3 — Get Involved

Audition for upcoming productions or join a crew in tech, costumes, sets, front-of-house, and more.
